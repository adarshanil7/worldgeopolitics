Geography: The Shield

Japan is an archipelago of 6,852 islands. It acts as a "lid" on China's access to the Pacific Ocean (First Island Chain). This makes it indispensable to US strategy but also a primary target for Beijing.

History & Resentment

1945: Total Defeat

The Atomic Trauma

Hiroshima & Nagasaki. Japan renounces war forever (Article 9). It creates a pacifist identity but leaves it dependent on the US for survival.

1990s: The Lost Decades

The Bubble Bursts

The economic miracle ends. Stagnation sets in. Meanwhile, China rises to replace Japan as Asia's leader, creating deep anxiety and resentment.
