1989

The Wall Falls

"Mr. Gorbachev, tear down this wall!"

I. The Divide II. The Mistake III. The Reunion

For 28 years, Berlin was the scar on the face of Europe. The Communists called it the "Anti-Fascist Protection Rampart." The West called it the "Wall of Shame."

East Germans who tried to escape were shot by guards in towers. It was the physical manifestation of the Iron Curtain.

II. The Mistake (Nov 9)

"As far as I know... effective immediately, without delay."

In 1989, protests rocked East Germany. The government decided to loosen travel restrictions. But spokesperson Günter Schabowski fumbled the announcement on live TV, implying the border was open *now*. Thousands rushed the checkpoints. Confused guards, unwilling to fire into the crowd, opened the gates.

III. The Reunion

Strangers hugged, danced on the wall, and chipped away pieces with hammers. The symbol of division had fallen. Within a year, Germany was reunified. The Cold War was effectively over.

SOURCES: "The Fall of the Wall" - Buckley
